## 摘要
我们报告了圆型限制性三体问题中希尔面的闭式表达式。该解 $φ(r,θ)$ 是在以主天体为中心的球坐标系中推导出来的，源于一个在分界线两侧各至多产生两个根的三次方程。文中精确地重现了著名的图案（蝌蚪形、马蹄形、花生形、洛希瓣以及希尔准球体）。

## Abstract
We report the closed-form expression for Hill's surfaces in the circular restricted three-body problem. The solution $φ(r,θ)$, derived in the primary-centric spherical coordinate system, is deduced from a cubic equation delivering at most two roots on each side of a separatrix. The famous patterns (tadpole, horseshoe and peanut shapes, Roche lobes and Hill's quasi-spheres) are exactly produced.
