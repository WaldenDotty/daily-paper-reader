## 摘要
太极计划是中国首个空间引力波探测任务，要求对其引力参考传感器（GRS）进行超高精度校准，以达到 $3\times10^{-15} \mathrm{\ m\ s^{-2}\ Hz^{-1/2}}$ 的目标灵敏度。由于推进剂消耗、热效应和电子设备老化等因素导致的随时间变化的比例因子漂移，以及检验质量（TM）与航天器之间的动态质心（c.m.）偏移，使得维持这一精度面临挑战。本文开发了一种先进的在轨校准框架，通过结合航天器机动和卡尔曼滤波器，同时估计 GRS 比例因子以及 TM 与航天器之间的质心偏移。通过施加周期性力矩信号以诱导受控的航天器角加速度，我们利用星敏感器和 GRS 读数来解耦耦合干扰，并实现了具有前所未有精度的双参数校准，比例因子误差低于 0.2%，质心偏移残差在 100 $\mathrmμ$m 以内，满足了太极二号的校准要求。该方法在不同的卫星配置下均具有鲁棒性。研究结果不仅确保了太极二号科学目标的可行性，还为太极三号等未来任务建立了一个可扩展的校准范式，在这些任务中，亚微米级的质心稳定性和超低噪声引力参考将至关重要。

## Abstract
The Taiji mission, a pioneering Chinese space-borne gravitational wave observatory, requires ultra-precise calibration of its gravitational reference sensors (GRSs) to achieve its targeted sensitivity of $3\times10^{-15} \mathrm{\ m\ s^{-2}\ Hz^{-1/2}}$. Maintaining this precision is challenged by time-varying scale factors drifts and dynamic center-of-mass (c.m.) offsets between the test masses (TMs) and spacecraft, driven by factors such as propellant consumption, thermal effects and aging electronics. This paper develops an advanced in-orbit calibration framework that simultaneously estimates the GRS scale factors and c.m. offsets between TMs and spacecraft through a combination of spacecraft maneuvers and Kalman filter. By applying periodic torque signals to induce controlled spacecraft angular accelerations, we leverage star tracker and GRS readouts to disentangle coupled disturbances and achieve dual-parameter calibration with unprecedented precision, with scale factors errors below 0.2\% and c.m. offsets residuals within 100 $\mathrmμ$m, satisfies the Taiji-2 calibration requirements. This method is robust across different satellite configurations. The results not only ensure the feasibility of Taiji-2's scientific objectives but also establish a scalable calibration paradigm for future missions such as Taiji-3, where sub-micrometer c.m. stability and ultra-low noise gravitational reference will be essential.
